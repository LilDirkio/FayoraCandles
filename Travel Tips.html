Travel tips

Burj Khalifa

When visiting Dubai, it is impossible not to have seen the Burj Khalifa. The tallest tower in the world can be seen from any location in Dubai. A visit to the tower itself promises stunning views of Dubai and its surroundings.

Take a look!
Kruger Park

One of the most famous and largest nature reserves in the world is the Kruger National Park in South Africa. The Kruger Park offers the unique opportunity to see the Big 5 in their own habitat and up close.

Take a look!
Kremlin

The Moscow Kremlin, or simply the Kremlin, is a fortified complex in the center of Moscow, overlooking the Moskva River to the south, Saint Basil's Cathedral and Red Square to the east, and the Alexander Garden to the west.

Take a look!
Comino

The island of Comino may be the smallest inhabited island in Malta. Yet there is a lot to do on the island with an area of only 3.5 km² and 3 inhabitants.

Take a look!
Alcúdia

Alcúdia has one of the whitest and widest beaches in Mallorca and is in possession of the blue flag award - the hallmark for beaches. The perfect place to relax for a while.

Take a look!
Lefkas

Are you looking for relaxation and tranquility? Visit Agios Nikitas on Lefkas. Here you get to know a Greek village at its best: idyllic, incredibly cozy and surrounded by sandy beaches.

Take a look!
